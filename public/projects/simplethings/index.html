Aisha Devi Music Video // Mixed Reality Experience

Created for the Simple Things Festival, while working at Zubr

Made in Unity, with AR Foundation

October 2019

As part of the Simple Things Festival, we developed a VR music for electronic artist A誰sha Devi and her track “I’m Not Always Where My Body Is”

We placed a 3D scan of A誰sha within a surreal, otherworldly landscape, and played different stems of the track around the space. Users could wander around, watching A誰sha move and seeing particle systems react to the different sounds.

This was a fun and interesting project to work on, working out how to create particle systems that would grow and change based on the volume of the different stems. And working with volumetric shaders to take the 3D scan of A誰sha and make her look awesome.

It was also challenging, as we were using GearVR headsets for the VR landscape, which utilised the camera and AR function to keep the users in the correct place based on tracking images around the area.
